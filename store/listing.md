# Play Store Listing — Tapcard

## App name
Tapcard — Digital Business Card

## Short description (≤80 chars)
Scan paper business cards, turn them into shareable digital cards with a QR code.

## Full description (≤4000 chars)
Tapcard turns the paper business cards piling up in your drawer into clean, shareable digital
cards — and lets you hand out your own with a single QR code. Point your camera at a card, Tapcard
reads the details on-device, you confirm them, and you get a polished digital card you can share,
save, or beam straight into anyone's address book.

EVERYTHING HAPPENS ON YOUR PHONE. The text recognition runs entirely on-device — no account, no
sign-up, no servers, no ads, no analytics. Your cards are stored locally and never uploaded.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Tap “Scan card” and line the business card up inside the frame.
2. Tapcard reads the name, title, company, phone, email, website and address on-device.
3. Review and tweak anything, then save.
4. Share your QR code, or tap “Save contact” to drop it straight into your phone's contacts.

FEATURES
• On-device card scanning — fast text recognition with no network and no API keys
• Automatic field detection — name, job title, company, phone, email, website, address
• Review & edit — fix anything before you save; nothing is locked in
• Shareable QR code — anyone can scan it to save the contact on iPhone, Android, Google or Outlook
• One-tap contact export — add the card straight to your address book as a vCard
• Quick actions — call, WhatsApp, email, or open the website right from the card
• My Cards — every card you create, stored privately on your device
• Add by hand — no card? Type the details in and still get a QR
• Beautiful Material design with automatic light & dark mode

PERMISSIONS
• Camera — only to photograph a business card so the details can be read on-device.

Tapcard is built for anyone who collects business cards at events, meetings and conferences and
wants them organised — privately, instantly, and without yet another login. Scan, share, done.
