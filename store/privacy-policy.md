# Privacy Policy — Tapcard

_Last updated: 16 June 2026_

Tapcard ("the app") is developed by Tertiary Infotech Academy Pte. Ltd. ("we", "us"). This policy
explains what the app accesses and how your data is handled. **In short: your data stays on your
device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share it.**

## Information the app uses

- **Camera.** Used solely to photograph a physical business card so its text can be read. The photo
  is processed **on your device** by Android's on-device text recognizer to extract contact fields.
  Card images are kept in the app's private cache for the scan and are **never uploaded** to us or
  any third party.
- **Card details.** The contact information you create (name, title, company, phone, email, website,
  address) is stored **locally** on your device using Android's private app storage. It is not backed
  up to us.

## Information we do NOT collect

We do not use accounts, advertising, analytics, tracking SDKs, or any servers. The app has no network
backend. We have no access to your camera, your scans, your cards, or any personal information.

## Data sharing

None. No data is shared with third parties because no data leaves your device. When you choose to
**share a QR code** or **save a contact**, you are handing the data to another app you pick (e.g.
your messaging or contacts app) through the standard Android share/insert sheet — that action is
initiated by you.

## Data retention and deletion

Cards remain on your device until you delete them. You can delete any card from its detail screen, or
uninstall the app to erase all stored data.

## Children

The app is a general-purpose productivity tool and is not directed at children under 13.
